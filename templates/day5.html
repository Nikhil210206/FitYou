Full Body Burn Workout

Warm-Up (5 minutes)

  • 2 minutes light jogging or high knees in place.
  • 1 minute arm circles (forward and backward).
  • 1 minute leg swings (forward and side-to-side).
  • 1 minute dynamic stretches: torso twists, cat-cow.

Workout Exercises (High Intensity & Cardio)

Jumping Jacks

Sets: 3

Reps: 30 seconds (continuous)

Tip: Keep a steady pace and focus on controlled movements.

Squats (Bodyweight or Goblet)

Sets: 3

Reps: 15-20

Tip: Chest up, back straight, push through your heels.

Push-ups (on knees or toes)

Sets: 3

Reps: Max reps

Tip: Keep your body in a straight line, engage your core.

Mountain Climbers

Sets: 3

Reps: 30 seconds (continuous)

Tip: Bring knees towards chest in a running motion, keep core tight.

Lunges (Alternating Legs)

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12 per leg

Tip: Ensure front knee stays behind toes, lower back knee towards ground.

Plank

Sets: 3

Hold: 45-60 seconds

Tip: Keep a straight line from head to heels, engage your glutes and core.

Burpees

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-15

Tip: Explode up, control the movement down, maintain good form.

Dumbbell Rows (Single Arm)

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12 per arm

Tip: Keep your back flat, pull dumbbell towards your hip, squeeze shoulder blade.

High Knees

Sets: 3

Reps: 30 seconds (continuous)

Tip: Drive knees up towards your chest, pump your arms.

Glute Bridges

Sets: 3

Reps: 15-20

Tip: Squeeze glutes at the top, lift hips off the floor, keep core tight.

Tricep Dips (using chair/bench)

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-15

Tip: Keep your back close to the chair, lower slowly, push through your palms.

Bicycle Crunches

Sets: 3

Reps: 15-20 per side

Tip: Touch opposite elbow to knee, keep lower back pressed into the floor.

Box Jumps (or Step-ups)

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12

Tip: Land softly, use your arms for momentum, step down carefully.

Dumbbell Shoulder Press

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12

Tip: Press dumbbells overhead, control the eccentric phase, keep core tight.

Reverse Crunches

Sets: 3

Reps: 15-20

Tip: Lift hips off the floor, bringing knees towards your chest, focus on lower abs.

Jump Squats

Sets: 3

Reps: 12-15

Tip: Explode up from the squat, land softly, absorb impact through knees.

Renegade Rows (with dumbbells)

Sets: 3

Reps: 8-10 per arm

Tip: Maintain a stable plank position, minimize hip sway while rowing.

Russian Twists (with or without weight)

Sets: 3

Reps: 15-20 per side

Tip: Lean back, lift feet off the ground (optional), twist from your torso.

Star Jumps

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-15

Tip: Start in a squat, explode up into a star shape, land softly.

Dumbbell Bicep Curls

Sets: 3

Reps: 10-12 per arm

Tip: Keep elbows tucked, squeeze at the top of the movement.

Cool Down (5-10 minutes)

  • Perform static stretches for major muscle groups: hamstring stretch, quad stretch, chest stretch, tricep stretch, shoulder stretch.
  • Hold each stretch for 20-30 seconds. Focus on deep breathing to aid recovery and relaxation.

Additional Tips

  • Rest: Keep rest periods short (30-45 seconds) between sets to maintain high intensity.
  • Progression: Increase intensity by adding more reps, sets, or decreasing rest time.
  • Form: Focus on proper form throughout all exercises to prevent injury and maximize effectiveness.
  • Hydration: Drink water consistently during and after your workout.
  • Listen to Your Body: If an exercise causes pain, stop and rest or modify it.
